Alexandr Simakov

Hello! My name is Alexander, I am 24 years old, and I'm a tattoo artist, and the owner of the tattoo studio Kingdom Of Shadows. In this beautiful and creative profession I have been for exactly 4 years. From childhood to today, I am actively engaged in drawing. So it turned out that in the family I am not one creative person, since my father is also a tattoo master, and a tattoo in it's like a tradition for my family. For me, a tattoo is not only making money, and not a hobby, but craft, and creating masterpieces that allow each of my clients to self-produce.


I work with different styles, and more precisely: "WhipShading, BlackWork, New School, Old School, Lettering, Grafica, Realism," I do and B/B so and color tattoos. I also worry and correct bad tattoos, as well as cover the scars. I develop individual sketch for order, special attention to each client, taking into account all the wishes to the very small details. I also do Cover Up, but I love it more and try to work on my personal sketches.

Instruments and materials

I work exclusively only professional, and expensive equipment such as Cheyene, Kwadron, Critical, World Famous, Eternal, Intenze, Raptor, Dt. Grits, Mark Eco Farm, etc. Sterility and dysenfection of the instrument and working surface on the first and most important place, with this in my studio very strictly. After each customer after of each session, all protective barriers are cleared and changed to new ones. After the session, I give a consultation on how to care for the tattoo, and I give a healing cream for free for tattoo.
