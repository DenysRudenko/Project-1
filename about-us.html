Alexandr Simakov

Hello! My name is Alexander, I am 24 years old, I'm a tattoo artist, and the owner of the tattoo studio Kingdom Of Shadows In this beautiful and creative profession I have been for exactly 4 years. Since the early childhood, I am actively engaged in drawing. I am not the only one creative in the family, because my father works in the same industry as I do. Tattooing is not only my job and hobby, but mostly a talent that helps my clients to express themselves.


I work in many styles, here is a list of them: "WhipShading, BlackWork, New School, Old School, Lettering, Grafica, Realism," I do and B/B so and color tattoos. I also correct tattoos, as well as covering up scars and old tattoos. I make individual sketches for clients, listen attentively to your ideas and take the very small details into consideration, happily answer all of your questions about tattoos and create comfortable atmosphere in the saloon.

Instruments and materials

I work only with professional equipment such as Cheyene, Kwadron, Critical, World Famous, Eternal, Intenze, Raptor, Dt. Grits, Mark Eco Farm, etc. Sterility and dysenfection of the instruments and working surface are taken very seriously and are always followed.


Before a cartridge session We unpack all the one-time-usage products needed for tattoo and cover of instruments and surfaces in front of every client. After each customer and each session , all protective barriers are changed to new ones. After the session, master gives a consultation on how to care for the tattoo, and gives a healing cream for free.
